🧠 FEATURE ENGINEERING

// WHERE WINNERS WIN
📈
Growth Rate
Percentage change in the 7-day moving average compared to 7 days prior. The strongest single predictor of outbreak trajectory.
growth_rate = ma_7.pct_change(7) × 100
〰️
7-Day Moving Average
Smooths out day-of-week reporting artifacts. Reveals the true underlying trend hidden in noisy daily counts.
ma_7 = daily_cases.rolling(7).mean()
⏱️
Doubling Time
Estimated days until cases double at current growth. Short doubling time → imminent surge. A key epidemiological metric.
ln(2) / ln(1 + growth_rate/100)
🔁
Lag Features
Autoregressive inputs: cases 1 day ago and 7 days ago. Captures the temporal dependency that makes epidemics predictable.
lag_1 = cases.shift(1)
lag_7 = cases.shift(7)
🚀
Case Acceleration
Second derivative of cases — rate of change of the growth rate. Detects whether an outbreak is accelerating or decelerating.
acceleration = growth_rate.diff()
⚠️
Z-Score Spike Detection
Flags anomalous spikes where the 7-day MA exceeds 2.5 standard deviations above the country's own mean. Early warning system.
z = (ma_7 − μ) / σ > 2.5
